@brief Buffers into the stream identified by `stream_id` an XRCE DELETE submessage. The submessage will be sent when `uxr_flash_output_stream` or `uxr_run_session` function are called. As a result of the reception of this submessage, the Agent will delete an XRCE entity.<br/>@param session       A uxrSession structure previously initialized.<br/>@param stream_id     The output stream identifier where the CREATE submessage will be buffered.<br/>@param object_id     The identifier of the XRCE entity.<br/>@return A `request_id` that identifies the request made by the Client. This could be used in the `uxr_run_session_until_one_status` or `uxr_run_session_until_all_status` functions.<br/>
